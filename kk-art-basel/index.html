3. KK for young thug as paintings

RS / 2018

For Scope at Art Basel Miami

Team KK was brought board to design and build an installation to accompany the artwork of "Young Thug As Paintings" at Scope art festival during Art Basel Miami 2018.

To build hype for the event, I was tasked with creating some press images that explained the show without revealing the installation designs themselves. This motion graphic, along with a few other variations were posted on social media, reaching over 15k views in just two days.

The specific graphic above also appeared in Women's Wear Daily in the days leading up to the event.
